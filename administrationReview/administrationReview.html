Rating Dos and Don'ts

Do

Discuss the professor's professional abilities including teaching style and ability to convey the material clearly.

Remember that these reviews are public and will directly affect the teacher's reputation.

Don't

Write strongly opinionated reviews of your faculties. Keep the overall experience with the faculty in mind and not specific experiences.

Note

Each review will be read by the UniConn team before being posted on the website.
