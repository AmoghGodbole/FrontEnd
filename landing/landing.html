UniConn - University Connect

We at UniConn.in are trying to solve a probem thats faced by every college in India - Communication. The aim of this website is to better the communication between teachers and students in colleges while also solving a few other challenges along the way.

We spoke to multiple teachers as well as students to come understand what exactly is wrong in the current system. The answer we came up with was simple - Visibility

We are not a teacher rating website but rather a “Teacher Branding Website”. Colleges should be known for the kind of teachers they have and not placements they get the students. This website is an honest attempt to change the outlook of students and improve the education system, from the ground up.

Imagine you just entered engineering and have no idea whats going on. Its a new campus, new city, new people, new everything and you don’t know whom to ask simple questions which will make your life simpler.

Imagine you have entered third year and need to choose a mentor for your project. You have to find a mentor by the end of the week and have no idea which teacher is good at what.

Imagine you have interest in a particular topic while in college and want to do a project related to it. You try to find a faculty in your college but nobody has expertise in that topic.

Imagine you have a faculty who is trying really hard to be better but needs a little perspective from a student to understand where they can improve.

Most of us faced situations like these while our time in engineering. If you havent yet, theres a good chance you will. UniConn is trying to solve all these problems, we just need your support.
